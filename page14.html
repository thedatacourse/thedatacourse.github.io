How TheDataCourse Works

TheDataCourse is an open-source course that offers you the flexibility to choose your own curriculum and learn as per your requirement. Being a self-paced course, follow the steps below to curate your own path to learning and development.

Getting Started

While TheDataCourse remains free and open-source, there are certain guidelines that we recommend you to follow to ensure you learn in a structured manner.
We believe learning needs to be goal-based, and the journey to completion should be fast-paced so that learners can complete the curriculum of their choice.
Please follow the steps below to start your learning journey.

01.

Enrollment

Enroll yourself in TheDataCourse here.
You will receive a request to be added as a Collaborator in the course, please accept the same.

02.

Create your space

Click here to download the coursework template. Follow the instructions here and upload your folder back into same repository mentioned.

03.

Choose curriculum

Choose the curriculum based on your learning goals. You may use TheDataCourse's GOLP or curate your own curriculum.

Goal Oriented Learning Path

Steps

TheDataCourse's Goal Oriented Learning Path is a selection of courses/topics tied together for learners to progress through, mastering a particular subject or program.
Whether you want to become a business analyst, a data analyst, a data scientist, a data engineer, a BI Analyst or simple a "No Code Analyst", you need the skills hiring managers seek for these positions.
This learning path will guide you so you can achieve your specific career goals, one training course at a time.
(We recommend using the GOLP if you are a beginner)

Goal Oriented Learning Path
1

Review the learning path and choose your Specialization

Click on the button above and review the GOLP.
Choose your specialization based on your learning and career goals.
2

Update coursework template

Fill in the required details in your coursework template and update the same in the repository. 
3

Start learning

Follow the GOLP Curriculum and complete each course.
(Use Bookmarks to keep a track of the course you will working on) 
4

Request for Certification

Once you have completed the curriculum, you can place a request for TheDataCourse Certification.
If you fulfill the completion criteria, we will grand you the certificate. 

Build Your Own Curriculum

Steps

TheDataCourse allows you to curate your own curriculum based on your learning and career goals. If you want to master a specific domain of data science, you can create your own curriculum using TheDataCourse course catalogue.

Course Catalogue
1

Review the Course Catalogue

Click on the button above and review the Course Catalogue to select the courses you want to add to our custom curriculum. If you are not able to find a course on the topic of your choice, get in touch with us and we will try to make it available for you.
2

Create your custom curriculum

Create your custom curriculum using the courses and update your coursework template in the repository with the course names. We recommend you to add the Setup course to your curriculum to ensure you have a structured learning path.
3

Start learning

Start your learning journey using your custom learning path.
(Use Bookmarks to keep a track of the course you will working on) 
4

Request for Certification

Once you have completed the curriculum, you can place a request for TheDataCourse Certification.
If you fulfill the completion criteria, we will grand you the certificate.
