TheDataCourse GOLP Curriculum

This Curriculum has been designed based on the
TheDataCourse GOLP.
Take up this curriculum if you want to learn the basics of TheDataCourse and make your way through a particular specialization based on your learning and career goals.

Features of this curriculum

Basic to Advanced

This curriculum covers everything from scratch but has a steep learning curve so that learners can specialize based on their goals.

In-depth courses

All courses within this curriculum are open-sourced by industry experts and cover every topic in much details.

Industry relevant projects

The curriculum also covers industry projects which will help learners build an amazing portfolio.

How this curriculum works?

If you are new to TheDataCourse or would like to know more how this curriculum works, please go through the How it works page to understand the process step-by-step.
