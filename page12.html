TheDataCourse Support System

TheDataCourse offers mentor support and collaborative learning in Slack groups to help your learning journey.
Although the TheDataCourse is open-source and completely free, you can book a mentor call to receive advice and guidance on your learning path. This is a paid service and details are mentioned below.
You can also join our Slack community to meet other learners who are also a part of TheDataCourse and working on their learning journey. Work together in groups and enjoy this process.
