What is TheDataCourse?

TheDataCourse is a holistic, business-oriented course on data science – no matter whether you’re more on the tech or the business side. This is an open-source course that covers both technical and functional aspects of working with data. The course will prepare you to participate in the data analysis process from start to end - from planning a data-based project to building a successful algorithm and finally deploying it in production, and everything in between (of course!).

Truly Open source course
